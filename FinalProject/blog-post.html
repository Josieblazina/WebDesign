About My Work

I'm a media arts student with a focus on graphic design and brand development. This portfolio collects work and projects I have made throughout my time studying media arts.

Graphic Design

My graphic design work explores how typography, color, and layout work together to communicate ideas clearly. I'm drawn to nostalgic, structured compositions that still feel warm and human, bringing physical elements and texture back into digital design.

Creative Concepts

I love to take an idea and develop it into something that feels well thought out and real. Branding and marketing are also very interesting to me.

What's Next

As I continue developing my skills, I'm looking forward to taking on more complex identity projects and building out this portfolio to contain more of my work.

Personal Portfolio

Continuing to build a portfolio site would let me showcase the projects I've built throughout my college courses. Like this assignment, it will include a homepage with a short introduction, a projects section using CSS Grid to display work samples, and an about me page. This feels like the most practical option since it's something I'd actually use.
